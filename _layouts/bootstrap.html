About

This is not another blog about new stuff in the tech industry. While blogging about all the new things I learn could be a good way to succeed as a tech worker, I decided to measure my success differently. To stay current in the fast paced tech industry, all one's spare time goes into creating toy projects. Toy projects that do the same things but are ever more complected. Therefore, I decided to try to do anything consequential. This blog describes my attempts to outpace the industry in solving some real problems, which is my measure of success.
